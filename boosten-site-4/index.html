Refund Policy
Last updated: May 2026 — Mayor Tech Inc
Wallet Top-Up

Wallet top-ups are generally non-refundable once credited to your account. If a payment was made in error and no orders have been placed, contact us within 24 hours and we will review your case.

Order Issues

If an order fails to start within 24 hours of placement, or if significant drop occurs within the refill window, you are eligible for a refill or credit back to your BoostEN wallet. Refunds are issued as wallet credit, not cash.

Virtual Numbers

Virtual number purchases are refunded automatically if no SMS is received within the timeout window. Cancellations initiated by the user before SMS receipt are also refunded to wallet balance.

How to Request

Email boostng.support@gmail.com with your order ID and a description of the issue. We respond within 24 hours.

Non-Refundable

Services that have been fully delivered, accounts banned for Terms of Service violations, and payments made more than 7 days ago are not eligible for refund.
