How to Use Boost Engine

Everything you need to know to place your first order in under 5 minutes.

1

Create Your Free Account

Go to boostengi.com and tap Get Started. Sign up with your email and password, or use Google sign-in for faster setup. No credit card needed to register.

2

Top Up Your Wallet

Tap the Wallet tab and choose an amount starting from $0.05. Pay with Flutterwave (card or bank transfer), PayPal, or Bitcoin. Prices show in your local currency automatically.

3

Place Your Order

Go to the Order tab, search for your platform (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube etc.), select a service, enter your profile or video link, set quantity and tap Place Order.

4

Track Your Order

Go to the Track tab to see your order progress in real-time. Most orders start within 0–30 minutes. You'll see the progress bar fill up as delivery completes.

5

Get Support Anytime

Tap the Support tab to chat with our AI assistant 24/7. For urgent issues, use the email link to reach the admin directly.
